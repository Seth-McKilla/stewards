Steward Report Cards

The Stewards of Gitcoin DAO play a vital role in driving the Gitcoin ecosystem forward through their work in governance and workstreams. In an effort to boost transparency the MMM-Workstream have created report cards for each Steward with metrics and links on their involvement and engagement in the DAO. To learn more and get involved - visit GitcoinDAO.com
